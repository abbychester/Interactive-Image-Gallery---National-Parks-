About National Parks:

National parks are areas of protected land with natural beauty, wildlife, and ecological significance, that have been established to preserve and showcase the country's heritage for future generations. They offer visitors opportunities for outdoor recreation, environmental education, and the chance to experience and appreciate diverse landscapes, from forests and mountains to deserts and wetlands. These parks play a vital role in conserving biodiversity and promoting environmental awareness.
